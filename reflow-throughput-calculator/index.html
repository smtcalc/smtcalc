Oven Parameters
cm
Heated zone length only. Not overall machine length. Check your oven datasheet.
sec
Use the total profile duration from your paste datasheet, oven entry to exit. For SnPb (Sn63/Sn62) typically 300–360 s. For SAC305 typically 240–300 s. TAL (time above liquidus) is only 45–60 s and is not the correct value here.
%
Ratio of board length to board pitch on the belt. 90% = ~10% gap between boards. Use 85–92% for most lines.
